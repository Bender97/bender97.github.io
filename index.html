Daniel Fusaro,
Ph.D.

        

  

I am a Post-Doctoral research fellow at the Department of Information Engineering at the University of Padua, supervised by Prof. Alberto Pretto. I also collaborate closely with Prof. Loris Nanni.

My research focus include 3D Point Cloud Semantic Segmentation, Traversability Analysis, and Agricultural Robotics.
